## Piano Lessons Minutes from Glenageary

Glenageary is one of the closest areas to our studio in Sandycove. Whether you're on the Glenageary Road, near the DART station, or up towards Sallynoggin, you're just a few minutes from Sandycove School of Music by car or DART.

We teach piano to children from age five and to adults of all levels — beginners, returners, and advanced students alike.

## Piano Lessons for Children in Glenageary

Many of our younger students come from Glenageary and the surrounding area. We start children from age five with 30-minute lessons that are fun, structured, and build a strong foundation in piano technique, reading music, and musical expression.

As children progress, they can move to longer lessons and begin working towards grade exams with RIAM, ABRSM, or Trinity if they choose. Exams are always optional — some children prefer to learn without the pressure of formal assessment, and that's perfectly fine.

For secondary school students, we also offer specialist [Leaving Cert Music preparation](/leaving-cert-music) and [Junior Cycle Music support](/junior-cycle-music).

## Piano Lessons for Adults

A growing number of our students are adults from Glenageary who are learning piano for the first time or returning after many years. Our adult lessons are relaxed, flexible, and focused on what you want to achieve — whether that's playing your favourite songs, working through the grades, or simply enjoying the creative outlet.

We have evening and Saturday slots available to fit around work and family life.

## Getting to the Studio from Glenageary

By car, we're a three to five minute drive from most of Glenageary. There's free street parking near the studio.

By DART, take the southbound train one stop from Glenageary to Sandycove and Glasthule — the studio is a short walk from the station.

## Pricing

Lessons start from €30 for 30 minutes, with packages of ten lessons available at a reduced rate. No commitment — just try your first lesson.
