## Piano Lessons Close to Dalkey and Killiney

Sandycove School of Music is just five minutes from Dalkey village, making us one of the closest piano schools for students in Dalkey and Killiney. Whether you're a parent looking for lessons for your child or an adult who has always wanted to learn, we're right on your doorstep.

## What Makes Our Lessons Different

Every lesson is one-on-one. We don't do group classes for instrumental tuition because we believe individual attention is the fastest way to progress. Your teacher will build a programme around your interests, ability, and goals — not a one-size-fits-all curriculum.

We cover classical, jazz, pop, and contemporary styles. If you have a favourite piece you've always wanted to play, we'll work it into your programme.

## Exam Preparation for Dalkey Students

Many of our Dalkey and Killiney students are working towards piano exams. We prepare students for:

- **RIAM** — Royal Irish Academy of Music, from initial grade to senior certificate
- **ABRSM** — Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music
- **Trinity College London** — graded exams and performance diplomas
- **Leaving Certificate Music** — the 50% performance component

Our students regularly achieve distinctions and merit awards across all boards.
