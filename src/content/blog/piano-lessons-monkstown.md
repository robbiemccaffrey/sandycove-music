## Piano Lessons a Short Walk from Monkstown

If you live in Monkstown or the surrounding area — Seapoint, Monkstown Farm, Salthill — you're just minutes from Sandycove School of Music. Our studio is a short walk from Monkstown village, making it one of the most convenient options for piano lessons in the area.

We teach children from age five and adults of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced students preparing for grade exams or the Leaving Cert.

## What We Offer Monkstown Students

Our lessons are one-on-one, so every session is tailored to you. Whether you want to learn classical piano, play jazz and pop, or prepare for RIAM, ABRSM, or Trinity exams, we build a programme around your goals.

Lessons are available in 30-minute, 40-minute, or one-hour formats, starting from €30. We also offer discounted packages of ten lessons for students who want to commit to regular weekly tuition.

## Getting Here from Monkstown

The studio is in Sandycove, less than a ten-minute walk from Monkstown village. If you're driving, there's free street parking nearby. The Sandycove and Glasthule DART station is also just a few minutes away on foot.

For students who prefer to learn from home, we offer online piano lessons via video call — same quality, same structure.

## Adults Are Welcome Too

Many of our Monkstown students are adults who are learning piano for the first time or picking it back up after years away. There's no pressure — you learn at your own pace, choose the music you enjoy, and exams are entirely optional.

Evening and weekend slots are available to fit around work.
